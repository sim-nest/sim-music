# sim-music

In one line: the whole world of music in SIM -- MIDI, theory, notation, synthesis, and sound, as first-class objects.

## What it gives you

The shared vocabulary of MIDI and the files that carry it, live in-and-out with a hardware-free test mode, a studio session you can arrange and bounce, deep pitch and chord and scale theory that names harmony in every school at once and translates between them, software synthesizers from FM to modular rigs, historical and modern tuning systems, spectral sound analysis, and rendering all the way to audio.
