# sim-lib-sound-gm

In one line: The standard General MIDI instrument and drum set, so program numbers map to the sounds everyone expects.

## What it gives you

This provides the General MIDI sound set. It includes the standard drum map, naming each percussion key and resolving its label, and a mapping of the 128 standard melodic programs onto concrete timbres in a timbre bank. In short, it makes the familiar General MIDI numbering -- where a given program means a particular instrument -- resolve to real sounds the SIM stack can play.

## Why you will be glad

- Get the expected instrument for any General MIDI program number.
- Resolve drum keys to named percussion sounds.
- Play standard MIDI files with the sounds they assume.

## Where it fits

This is the standard-instrument catalogue of the SIM sound family. It feeds the sound bridge a ready bank of timbres keyed to the General MIDI convention, so ordinary MIDI files that rely on that numbering sound the way their makers intended.
